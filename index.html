About the Project

وَمَن يُعَظِّمْ شَعَائِرَ اللَّهِ

Every year, the names of certain Imams are spoken only in passing — their hadiths unread, their sacrifices undiscussed. TLKI was born from a simple belief: that every Imam (as) deserves to be known. We host majalis, share daily hadiths, and create resources so no Imam's legacy remains in the shadows.
